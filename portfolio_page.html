BLUE CHIP

Commercial Real Estate, LLC.

Blue Chip is a Commercial Real Estate LLC serving Chicagoland. Started in 2008, the firm specializes in retail brokerage and development services, while providing comprehensive brokerage services in tenant and product representation, investment sales, and property acquisitions.

5/2018

Goal

Using an outdated website with missing SEO, website traffic data, and listing pages, Blue Chip Commercial Real Estate, LLC. needed an online overhaul. Beyond setting up tracking and optimization, the site would need user privileges for brokers to update and manage listings on Wordpress’ content management system.

Click here to rotate

Results

The final website consisted of a landing page to improve company branding and site direction, a listing page which linked individual properties to their own pages, a news page for brokers to update visitors with relevant news while building an online presence, and a contact page for visitors to take action and grow business. The site is now managed without the need of technical skill, and dynamically links properties to features like Google Maps and printable PDFs. Online tracking is in place and currently undergoing analysis.

vom SCHLOSS BILLIMACK

Working Dogs

Vom Schloss Billimack Working Dogs is a world championship competing working dog kennel. Working over 20 years with nationally recognized Shepherds in the sport of I.P.O. (formerly Schutzhund), the kennel continues to compete in trials consisting of tracking, obedience, and protection.

4/2018

Goal

The kennel had a strong presence on social media and hoped to expand towards a professional site of their own, presenting their information and updates to the working dog community.

Click here to rotate

Results

Using existing high quality media, Dart adapted and designed a Wordpress site for vom Schloss Billimack. The site consists of a landing page for brand awareness featuring a new logo. The purpose of the site is to convey kennel news to those interested in the sport, so a page featuring past and present dogs and their accomplishments now engages visitors. With an already strong social media presence, the site also features a news section where users can adapt social content to their own site.
